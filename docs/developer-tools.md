### IntelliJ IDEA Ultimate

JetBrains' commercial IDE used for development across the stack — TypeScript /
JavaScript, Node, the React Native app, and the (future) NestJS API, plus
database, Docker, and HTTP/OpenAPI tooling. **One seat** for the developer.

- **Licensing:** because the **organization pays**, this is a **commercial /
  organization** subscription (not a personal license, which JetBrains requires
  the individual to fund). One seat = one concurrent developer.
- **Pricing (per seat):** ~**US $599 / yr** in year 1; JetBrains applies a
  **continuity discount** on renewal (~US $479 yr 2, ~US $359 yr 3+).
- ⚠️ **Confirm current pricing** and the CAD/USD rate:
  [JetBrains IntelliJ IDEA pricing](https://www.jetbrains.com/idea/buy/).
  **Discounts:** JetBrains offers **free/discounted** licenses for open-source,
  startups, students/teachers, and some **non-profits/charities** — worth
  checking eligibility.

> Adding developers = additional seats at the same per-seat rate.

## Canadian tax treatment — 100% deductible

A developer-tool **subscription** is an **ordinary operating expense incurred to
produce the organization's software, and is fully (100%) deductible in the year
incurred.**

- **Current expense, not capital** — an annual subscription, deducted in full in
  the year incurred (Income Tax Act **s. 9** / **s. 18(1)(a)**), not a capital
  asset depreciated via CCA.
- **No personal-use portion** — used wholly for the organization's software work.
- **GST/HST** — if registered, tax charged is recoverable as an **input tax
  credit**; the net cost excludes recoverable tax.
- Keep the JetBrains invoices as evidence.
