# Canadian / BC business & First Nations lookup resources

**Purpose:** A research reference — where to find public records about
Canadian businesses (especially BC-registered and Indigenous-owned), First
Nations, and government contracts/grants. What's public, what isn't, and the
authoritative places to look.

> **Rule of thumb (Canada):** Private corporations do **not** file financial
> statements publicly. Public companies (TSX/TSXV/CSE) file financials via
> **SEDAR+**. For a private `Ltd.` the registry tells you it exists, who the
> directors are, and where the registered office is — **not** revenue or
> profit. Public-money flows (procurement contracts, grants/contributions) are
> the main way real dollar figures become visible.

## Government contracts & open data (start here)

The Open Government portal is the single best entry point for **government
contract and grant lookups** — both the federal proactive-disclosure dataset
and many provincial/municipal datasets are indexed here.

| Source | What you get | Link |
|---|---|---|
| **search.open.canada.ca** | Unified search across Government of Canada open data — contracts, grants, departments, datasets. Search a supplier name and see every disclosed federal contract by department, value, date, scope. | <https://search.open.canada.ca/> |
| **Open Canada — Proactive Disclosure of Contracts** | All federal contracts **> $10K** by supplier — date, value, department, description, contract type | <https://search.open.canada.ca/contracts/> |
| **Open Canada — Grants & Contributions** | Federal grants/contributions (incl. ISC, CIRNAC, ISED, PrairiesCan, PacifiCan funds — often the channel for funds to bands and Indigenous businesses) | <https://search.open.canada.ca/grants/> |
| **Open Canada — Datasets** | Raw datasets behind the search portal | <https://open.canada.ca/data/en/dataset> |
| **BC Bid — Contract Awards** | Provincial & Crown-corp contract awards (BC Hydro, MoTI, ICBC, BCLC, etc.) — supplier, $ value, scope | <https://www2.gov.bc.ca/gov/content/bc-procurement-resources/bc-bid-resources/bc-bid-user-guides/bc-bid-public-portal-guide/contract-awards> · <https://www.bcbid.gov.bc.ca/> |
| **MERX** | Federal + many provincial active and historical tender opportunities | <https://www.merx.com/> |
| **CivicInfo BC — Current Opportunities** | BC municipal & local-government tenders | <https://www.civicinfo.bc.ca/bids> |
| **BC Open Data Catalogue** | Provincial open data (incl. some contract & grant datasets) | <https://catalogue.data.gov.bc.ca/> |

## Tips

- **Search a supplier across both federal & provincial** — `search.open.canada.ca`
  for federal contracts; **BC Bid contract awards** for provincial/Crown.
  Municipal contracts are scattered across city open-data portals.
- **Federal contracts are disclosed at >$10K** (threshold may change — verify).
- **Self-reported directories** (ISC IBD, city business directories, CCAB
  free listings) are exactly that — verify ownership claims independently if it
  matters (CCAB **certified** CAB listings are audited).
- **What you almost certainly won't find publicly for a private `Ltd.`:**
  revenue, profit, exact contract backlog, or audited financial statements.
- **For First Nations governments** (vs. their member-owned businesses), check
  the **First Nations Financial Transparency Act** disclosures and FMB
  certification status; not all Nations file consistently.

> This document is not legal, tax, or due-diligence advice. For material
> procurement or partnership decisions, use paid registry products (BC Company
> Summary, PPSA search, CSO documents) and consult counsel.
