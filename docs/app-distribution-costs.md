# App distribution cost record — Apple & Google

**Prepared:** 2026-05-22
**Purpose:** Cost basis for the developer accounts needed to publish the Skin
Tyee app, kept as supporting documentation for business-expense / tax
substantiation.

> The app (`@skintyee/app`) distributes to **iOS via TestFlight / App Store** and
> **Android via Google Play** (see [`testing-strategy.md`](testing-strategy.md)).
> Publishing to either store requires a paid developer account.

## What the expense is for

A developer account with each store, required to build, test (TestFlight /
Play internal testing), and publish the app:

| Account | Cost | Cadence | Notes |
|---|--:|---|---|
| **Apple Developer Program** | **US $99 / yr** (~CAD $135) | **annual** (recurring) | Required for TestFlight + App Store. An **Organization** account (recommended) needs a **D-U-N-S number** for the Nation |
| **Google Play Developer** | **US $25** (~CAD $34) | **one-time** registration | Per developer account; not recurring |

> ⚠️ **Confirm current pricing** — Apple often bills in local currency
> (CAD ~$119–135/yr) and Google's fee is a one-time US $25. Authoritative
> sources: [Apple Developer Program](https://developer.apple.com/programs/) ·
> [Google Play Console signup](https://play.google.com/console/signup).
> **Possible fee waivers:** Apple waives the annual fee for eligible
> **non-profit / government** organizations via the
> [fee waiver program](https://developer.apple.com/support/membership-fee-waiver/);
> Google also has nonprofit programs. If approved, the Apple cost can be **$0**.

## Cost basis

| Item | Cost (CAD, approx.) | When |
|---|--:|---|
| Apple Developer Program | ~$135 / yr | every year |
| Google Play Developer | ~$34 | once (setup) |

So **~$135/yr recurring** (Apple) plus a **one-time ~$34** (Google) at setup —
or **$0/yr** for Apple if the non-profit fee waiver is approved.

## Canadian tax treatment — 100% deductible

Both are **ordinary operating expenses incurred to run the organization's app and
are fully (100%) deductible.**

- The **Apple annual fee** is a recurring **current expense**, deducted in full
  in the year incurred (Income Tax Act **s. 9** / **s. 18(1)(a)**).
- The **Google one-time $25** is a small current expense, also deductible in the
  year incurred (de minimis — not a capital asset).
- **GST/HST:** if registered, tax charged is recoverable as an **input tax
  credit**; the net cost excludes recoverable tax.
- Keep the Apple/Google receipts as evidence.

> **Not tax advice.** Internal cost record; confirm with the organization's
> accountant / CRA, including eligibility for the Apple/Google non-profit waivers.
