## What the expense is for

for the website and email and that the app/ERP run under:

- **`skintyee.ca`** — primary (website, `app.skintyee.ca`, `@skintyee.ca` email)

## ⚠️ Don't lose the domain

Unlike a per-seat subscription there's no "offboarding" cost, but the domain is
**single points of failure** for the website **and** email:

- **Auto-renew must stay ON**, billed to a card that won't expire silently. An
  expired `.ca`/`.org` takes down the site and `@skintyee.ca` email and can be
  re-registered by someone else.
- Keep **GoDaddy account access in 1Password** with **2FA on**, and the
  **registrant contact** on an org-controlled mailbox (update it when staff leave).
- Keep **registrar lock ON** to block unauthorized transfers.

## Canadian tax treatment — 100% deductible

Domain registration/renewal is an **ordinary operating expense incurred to run
the organization, and is fully (100%) deductible in the year it is incurred.**

- **Current expense, not capital** — an annual renewal fee, deducted in full in
  the year incurred (Income Tax Act **s. 9** / **s. 18(1)(a)**), not a capital
  asset depreciated via CCA.
- **No personal-use portion** — used wholly for the organization.
- **GST/HST** — if registered, tax charged is generally recoverable as an
  **input tax credit**, so the net cost excludes recoverable tax.
- **Record-keeping** — keep the GoDaddy receipts as evidence.
