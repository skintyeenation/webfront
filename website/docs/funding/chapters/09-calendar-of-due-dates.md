# Calendar of Due Dates

This chapter (Chapter 9 of the ISC BC Region 2024–2025 Program Guide) is a
consolidated **Calendar of Data Collection Instrument (DCI)** reporting dates and
**Proposal / Application / Work plan (PAW)** submission dates for all Indigenous
Services Canada (ISC) funding streams in the BC Region. It is a date reference
only — it does **not** restate each program's purpose, eligibility, or contacts
(those live in the program-specific chapters). Entries below are grouped by ISC
program area so band administrators can see, per program, **how to apply (PAW # +
due date)** and **what to report (DCI # + due date)**.

> **Important reminder:** Dates are current as of publication and are **not**
> comprehensive. Changes are communicated through the regular call-package
> process. In any variance, your **ISC funding agreement, the Reporting Guide,
> and ISC Program Manuals take precedence** over this calendar.

**Template sources**

- PAW (Proposal / Application / Work plan) templates: <https://www.sac-isc.gc.ca/eng/1571829044381/1571829074923>
- DCI (reporting) templates — Reporting Guide: <https://sac-isc.gc.ca/eng/1573764124180/1573764143080>

**Reading the dates:** the calendar runs Dec 2023 → Aug 2025. Most **DCI reports
due in spring/summer 2024** cover the **2023-2024** fiscal year; the same DCIs
recur a year later (spring/summer 2025) for **2024-2025**. PAW applications are
generally for the **2024-2025** funding year.

## New Fiscal Relationship

**What to report (DCI):** **DCI 333315098** New Fiscal Relationship Grant Results
Report — due **Jul 29 (2024 & 2025)**. Covers the **Income Assistance, Assisted
Living, and Community Infrastructure & Housing** sections. For the Housing section,
contact the regional office for a pre-populated regional report.

---

### Key contacts

The calendar itself lists no named contacts. For several reports — **Employee
Benefits (DCI 41784)**, **Community Infrastructure & Housing (DCI 41701)**, and the
**New Fiscal Relationship Housing section (DCI 333315098)** — band administrators
must **contact the ISC BC regional office for a pre-populated regional report**.
For all other program-specific contacts, eligibility, and purpose details, refer
to the relevant program chapter of the 2024–2025 BC Region Program Guide.
